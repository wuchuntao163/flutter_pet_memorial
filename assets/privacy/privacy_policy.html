哈基米倒数日 隐私政策

Platforms: Android & iOS
Effective date: June 9, 2026
Last updated: June 9, 2026

Welcome to 哈基米倒数日 (the "App"). We value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device identifier: a device UUID generated for anonymous sign-in and account identification.
  • Account & contact info: phone number and verification code when you bind your phone for identity verification and cloud sync.
  • Pet & memorial data: pet type, name, avatar, memorial titles, dates, reminders, backgrounds, font styles, etc. Data is stored locally and uploaded when cloud sync is enabled.
  • Images & media: photos you choose for pet avatars, custom backgrounds, saved memorial images, or sharing.
  • Widget data (iOS): limited pet display data shared with the Home Screen widget via App Group.
  • Local preferences: language, cloud sync toggle, desktop floating pet status (Android), and other settings.
  • Network logs: basic request/error logs for service stability.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve memorial recording, pet companion, reminders, sharing, and widgets;
  • Phone binding, cloud sync, and account management;
  • Save generated memorial images to your album with your permission;
  • Send local memorial reminders with your authorization;
  • Ensure security, troubleshoot issues, and comply with applicable laws.

4. Storage & Sharing

  • Local storage: some data is stored on your device.
  • App Group (iOS): widget display data only.
  • Server storage: when cloud sync or phone binding is enabled, data is transmitted to our servers (pet.laowaidrivetest.com).
  • Third parties: when you share content, the system share sheet may open WeChat, Xiaohongshu, or other apps. We do not sell your personal information.
  • Retention: data is kept while you use the service and as required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • Access and update pet profile and memorial data;
  • Disable cloud sync and sign out;
  • Manage permissions in system settings;
  • Remove the Home Screen widget (iOS) to stop App Group display;
  • Contact us via in-app support to request account/data deletion.

6. Children

The App is intended primarily for adults. Minors should use the App with guardian consent and guidance.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact Us
